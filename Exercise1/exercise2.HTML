How to swim:

  1. Be relaxed with the water
  2. Have someone experinced to hold you very carefully through the pool.
  3. Try to simulate the way someone experinced would swim.
  4. Practice! try to enroll in a club were you can swim there regularly.
  5. after some time, you will feel confident and you will try slowly to swim by yourself.

Important notes!

Do not try to learn everything in one day!
Learning how to swim will is a short journy not a long one! try to perfect your body movement.

Do not ever attempt to learn how to swim alone!
You must have some people that are experinced enough around just in case.
